Crystals Collector

Instructions:

  • You will be given a random Target Number between 19 - 120 at the beginning of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it, and changes at the beginning of each new game. Each value will be between 1 - 12.
  • You win the game by matching your Current Score to the Target Number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Target Number.
